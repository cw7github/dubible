What Changed?

  • Before: flex items-end gap-3 with pb-0.5 on Chinese text
  • After: flex items-baseline gap-3 (removed pb-0.5)

Why This Fixes the Artifact

  • items-end aligns to the bottom edge of the flex container, not the text baseline
  • With leading-none (line-height: 1), the text box tightly wraps the font
  • The pb-0.5 padding creates a small gap below the character
  • For characters with bottom strokes (like 耶 and 家), this gap appears as a visual artifact/line
  • items-baseline aligns text by their natural baseline, eliminating the gap
