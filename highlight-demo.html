Key Improvements

  • No Background Rectangle: Text remains inline with natural flow
  • Color Transformation: Characters change to theme-specific accent colors
  • Glowing Effect: Subtle text-shadow creates luminous quality
  • Smooth Animation: 600ms transition with gentle spring easing
  • Font Weight Change: Slight boldness (400→500) adds emphasis
  • Scale Effect: Characters grow 5% mid-animation for attention
  • Pinyin Coordination: Pinyin syllables illuminate with characters
  • Theme Harmony: Each color palette designed for its background
